Introducing Balance ECO
  • Monthly liquidity gain: 10-30%.
  • The investment threshold is V1～V4, and the income for each level is as follows: V1 capped at 2x, V2 capped at 2.5x, V3 capped at 3x, V4 capped at 3.5x.
  • Bonus pool: 3% of daily incoming funds will flow into the bonus pool, 15% will be issued and 85% will automatically roll over to the next round.
  • Insurance Pool: 1.5% of the funds flow into the insurance pool, 70% is awarded to the global nodes and 30% is equally divided between V4.
  • Perpetual pool: 3.5% of the funds flow into the perpetual pool, when the first round of liquidity is not enough to support, the blockchain will automatically inject a new pool of contracts, forever continue the operation of Balance ECO, at the same time 80% of the ECO Token pool will be injected into the operation of the main contract circulation.
  • Global Nodes: Provide strong liquidity for Balance ECO and enjoy a global dividend for new capital coming in.
